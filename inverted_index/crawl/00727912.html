The world's three largest strategy consulting firms

The Big Three or MBB is the name colloquially given to the three large strategy consulting firms . They are considered to be the most prestigious firms in the management consulting industry. [ 1 ] In terms of employees, McKinsey & Company is the largest and leads by revenue. McKinsey is followed by Boston Consulting Group and Bain & Company , respectively.

The firms

[ edit ]

The three consulting firms widely regarded as constituting the Big Three (or MBB) are McKinsey & Company , Boston Consulting Group , and Bain & Company .

These three firms are among the world's largest strategy consulting firms by revenue . Their latest publicly available data is summarized in the table below: [ 2 ]

McKinsey & Company

[ edit ]

This firm was founded in Chicago by James O. McKinsey in 1926. The firm has grown significantly since then, establishing 104 offices located in 60 countries as of 2014. [ 12 ] McKinsey & Company has been voted number one in "The Best Consulting Firms: Prestige" list of the Vault.com career intelligence website consecutively for 14 years since 2002. [ 13 ] [ 14 ] The firm currently serves two-thirds of the Fortune 1000 . Their areas of expertise include management , organization , operations and IT . [ 15 ]

Boston Consulting Group

[ edit ]

BCG was founded in 1963 by Bruce D. Henderson , a former Arthur D. Little consultant and a Vanderbilt University and Harvard Business School alumnus. [ 16 ] Starting out with only two consultants, the firm quickly grew. As of 2021, it employs 25,000 people in over 90 offices in more than 50 countries. [ 17 ] [ 18 ] BCG also has been consistently featured in Consulting magazine's "Best Firms to Work For" lists since 2001. [ 19 ] Their areas of expertise include corporate development , business growth and innovation . [ 20 ]

Bain & Company

[ edit ]

Bain was founded by Bill Bain in 1973 after leaving BCG. The firm pioneered private equity consulting and the fund Bain Capital was founded by partners from Bain & Company and led by Mitt Romney . According to the company's website in 2017, it had 65 offices in 40 countries. [ 21 ] Their areas of expertise include private equity , mergers and acquisitions and retail . [ 22 ] Bridgespan , a non-profit consultancy, was spun out of Bain in the early 2000s by three former Bain employees. [ 23 ] The firm has been ranked multiple times as one of the "Best Places to Work" by Glassdoor , and has placed in the top five for the last decade. [ 24 ]
