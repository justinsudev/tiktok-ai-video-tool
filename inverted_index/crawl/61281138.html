Incumbent
Lyudmila Petkova

Reports to Prime Minister

Website www.government.bg

The Deputy Prime Minister of Bulgaria ( Bulgarian : Заместник министър-председател на България ) is the deputy of the Prime Minister of Bulgaria and a member of the Government of Bulgaria . They can also be one of the Ministers of Bulgaria and the position could be held by multiple people at once. [ 1 ]
