Clonlara School is private alternative school located in Ann Arbor, Michigan, United States.

History

[ edit ]

Clonlara was founded by Pat Montgomery in 1967 as a private day school. [ 1 ]

Home schooling program

[ edit ]

The school offers a correspondence program for home schooled students. [ 1 ] As of 2013, about 5000 students are enrolled in the correspondence program, but only 50 attend classes on the campus. [ 2 ]
