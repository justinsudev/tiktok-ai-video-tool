Scalr is an American cloud computing company specializing in automation and collaboration software for Terraform . Scalr helps technical teams of all sizes deploy IT resources using infrastructure as code while maintaining policies around cost, security, and compliance.

History

[ edit ]

Scalr was founded by Sebastian Stadil in 2007 as an organizational model to help standardize processes across IT teams and later became a hybrid cloud management platform that was incorporated in 2011 and has grown rapidly. [ 1 ] Scalr raised $7.35 Million in Series A funding [ 2 ] from OpenView Venture Partners in 2016. Scalr officially released its second product in 2020, a remote operations backend for Terraform to help IT infrastructure professionals deal with the complexity of managing Terraform at scale.
